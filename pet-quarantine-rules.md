# Pet Quarantine Rules Guide

Traveling internationally with pets may require compliance with quarantine regulations depending on the destination country.

PetDocify helps pet owners organize travel documentation and prepare for international pet transport requirements.

## What Is Pet Quarantine?

Pet quarantine is a mandatory isolation period required by some countries before allowing pets to enter.

Quarantine requirements are usually based on:

- Country of origin
- Rabies risk classification
- Vaccination status
- Microchip verification
- Veterinary health documentation

## Common Requirements

Pet owners may need:

- Rabies vaccination certificates
- ISO-compliant microchips
- Veterinary health certificates
- Import permits
- Blood titer test results
- Travel timelines and booking confirmations

## Countries With Strict Quarantine Policies

Some destinations may have stricter entry requirements for pets, including:

- Australia
- New Zealand
- Singapore
- Japan
- United Kingdom

Travel rules can change frequently depending on government regulations.

## Prepare Before Traveling

Before booking travel:

1. Verify destination import rules
2. Confirm airline pet policies
3. Schedule veterinary appointments
4. Prepare vaccination records
5. Organize travel documentation
